Quick-reference card

The whole month on one screen — screenshot it for your next shoot.

SETTINGS

4K / 24 fps, grid on, AE/AF lock every shot, expose for highlights & faces.

STABILITY

Elbows in, brace on something, 10-second shots, exhale slowly.

EVERY SCENE

Five-shot method: hands CU · face CU · wide · OTS · creative.

COMPOSITION

Eyes on the top third, lead room, shoot through foreground for depth.

LIGHT

Subject at 45° to a window; outdoors use golden hour or open shade; at night, pools of light with the source in frame.

SOUND

Within 1 metre, kill the hum, block the wind, record room tone.

EDIT GRAMMAR

One side of the 180° line, cut on action, vary sizes, hard cuts only.

THE HABIT

Never shoot without watching it back the same day. That's the whole trick.
